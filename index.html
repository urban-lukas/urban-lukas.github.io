About Me

Hi, my name is Lukas Urban, I am 21 and I make game engines.

Since childhood I was fascinated about technology around me. By the years this fascination gradually grew into a hobby. I've always been interested in playing videogames so I naturally wanted to know how to make one. I came across several game engines but I wanted to understand how they work beneath the surface. This is where my passion for making game engines and game related software started. I went to the Secondary School of Electrical Engineering V Úžlabině in Prague and eventually graduated in 2022. I currently study Computer Science at Czech Technical University in Prague.
