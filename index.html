About Me

Hi, my name is Lukas Urban, I am 20 and I make game engines.

Since childhood I was fascinated about technology around me. By the years this fascination gradually grew into a hobby. I went to the Secondary School of Electrical Engineering V Úžlabině in Prague and started to learn about all kinds of technology. I learned how to program in C/C++/C#. I eventually graduated in 2022. I've always been interested in playing games so I naturally wanted to know how to make one. I came across several game engines but I wanted to understand how they work beneath the surface. This is where my passion for making game engines and game related software started.
